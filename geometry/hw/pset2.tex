\begin{prop}
    Define $F:\R^3\to\R^4$ by $F(x,y,z)=(x^2-y^2,xy,xz,yz)$. Since $F(v)=F(-v)$, this gives a well-defined map $\tilde F:\RP^2\to\R^4$.
    This is, in fact, an embedding.
\end{prop}
